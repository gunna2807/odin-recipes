Meat Loaf

Best Meatloaf ever

A few years ago I started experimenting with different meatloaf recipes to create my own version.
I finally came up with the best meatloaf I have ever made, and I want to share it.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/4 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 pound of extra-lean ground beef
  • 3 slices of bread, toasted and crumbed
  • 7 buttery round crackers, crushed
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 3 1/2 tablespoons sour cream
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons Worchestershire sauce
  • 1 (15oz) can tomato sauce, divided
  • 1/4 cup milk, optional
  • 3 tablespoons ketchup

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Melt the butter in a skillet over a medium heat and cook the onion and garlic for 5 minutes or until the onion is tender. Remove from heat and season with salt and pepper.
  3. In a large bowl mix the onion, garlic, beef, crumbled bread, crushed crackers, egg, sour cream, Worcestershire sauce and 1/2 of the tomato sauce.
    Gradually stir in the milk one teaspoon at a time until mixture is moist but not soggy. Transfer the mixture to a 5x9 inch loaf pan.
  4. Bake uncovered in the preheated oven for 40 minutes. Increase the oven temperature to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) and continue baking for 15 minutes,
    to an internal temperature of 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
  5. In a small bowl mix together the remaining tomato sauce and ketchup. Pour over the top of the meatloaf and continue baking for 10 minutes.
