Easy Mexican Casserole

Easy Mexican casserole

This is an easy and very tasty dish. Serve with chips, salsa and a green salad.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 cups salsa
  • 1 (16oz) can chili beans, drained
  • 3 cups tortilla chips, crushed
  • 2 cups sour cream
  • 1 (2oz) can sliced olives, drained
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh tomato
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, cook ground beef until no longer pink. Stir in salsa, reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes
    or until liquid is absorbed. Stir in beans and heat through.
  3. Spray a 9x13 inch baking dish with cooking spray. Spread crushed tortilla chips in the dish and then spoon beef mixture over the chips.
    Spread the sour cream over the beef and sprinkle the olives, green onion and tomato over the sour cream. Top with Cheddar cheese.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes or until hot and bubbly.
