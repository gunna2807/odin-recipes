Tomato Pork Loin Chops

Pork Loin Chops in a homemade tomato sauce

Rich, tasty and easy to make with delicious on-hand ingredients! Serve with egg noodles.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil, divided
  • 4 boneless pork loin chops, pound thin
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 teaspoons chilli powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennell seeds, crushed
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 fluid ounces water
  • 1 teaspoon Worchestershire sauce
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a skillet over high heat and quickly brown the chops on both sides. Transfer the chops to a platter and keep warm.
  2. Reduce heat to medium and cook the onion until tender. Mix in the sugar, chili powder, fennel, red pepper flakes and oregano.
    Stir in the tomato sauce, water and Worcestershire sauce then bring to the boil.
  3. Return the pork chops to the skillet. Cover and reduce heat to low and cook for 10 minutes.
    Remove cover and continue cooking for 15 minutes or to desired doneness.
